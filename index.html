Guess the Coutry

How To Play

You can guess in two ways:
  • Click directly on a country on the map
  • Type the country name in the input field and press Guess button or Enter on your keyboard
Understanding Feedback
After each guess, the guessed country will be colored based on the distance to the target country. Red means you're close, purple means medium distance, and blue means you're far away.
Close Medium Far
Game Controls:
  • Input Field: Type country names to make guesses
  • Guess Button: Submit your typed country name
  • New Game Button: Start a fresh game with a new target country
  • Give Up Button: Start a fresh game and show the answer of previous game
